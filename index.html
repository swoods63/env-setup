The Perfect Banana Split

This banana split is my favorite dessert. It has the perfect balance of Ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • The bowl
  • A Banana
  • 3 scoops of ice cream
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream
  • A maraschino cherry

Directions

  1. Place 3 scoops of ice cream in your bowl
  2. Slice a banana in half
  3. Place the halves on either side of the ice cream
  4. Cover in whipped cream
  5. Drizzle chocolate syrup over the top
  6. Garnish with a cherry
  7. Enjoy the banana split!
